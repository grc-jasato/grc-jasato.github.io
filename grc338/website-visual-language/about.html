Meow Meow Munchies is a cafe where customers can interact with cats while enjoying their coffee or tea. Our menu consists of our favorite Japanese foods and drinks. Our adorable cats are from shelters and were chosen because of their adorable, playful character. Our logo is a Maneki-neko cat. She is a Japanese figurine that is to bring good luck and fortune.

The cats are free to roam around the cafe, and customers can pet them, play with them, or simply observe them while they go about their daily routines. Everyone who chooses to interact are given a bag of treats to hand out to the adorable cats!

We also offer a variety of snacks and drinks, including traditional Japanese appetizers. Our cafes even have special cat-themed menus with items such as "cat-shaped" cookies or lattes with cute designs made from foam.

The staff at our cafe are often trained to ensure the safety and well-being of both the cats and the customers. We are also knowledgeable on the different breeds of cats and their behaviors, and can provide information to customers who are interested in learning more about them.

Overall, Meow Meow Munchies Cat Cafe is a wonderful and relaxing experience for anyone who loves cats or simply wants to try something new and unique. To donate to our shelter and others in the area, ask our workers for more information!
