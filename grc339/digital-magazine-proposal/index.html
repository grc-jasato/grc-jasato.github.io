Introduction

Pulse

Pulse is a magazine dedicated to spotlighting influencial and talented women in the music industry. Women are the pulse of the music world, bringing energy and creativity. Pulse captures the heartbeat of the music scene, admiring these inspiring women and their diverse contributions. The purpose of this magazine is to highlight under-represented women who made and are continuing to make history in the music industry.
